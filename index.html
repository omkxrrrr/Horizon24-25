Horizon Fest is an annual tech event by the CS-IT Department of Kirti M. Doongursee College, established in 2002. Inspired by the motto “Unity is Strength,” it unites students through technology with events like Conexa, a networking platform, and coding competitions. Horizon fosters creativity, innovation, and collaboration, making it a must-attend for tech enthusiasts.
